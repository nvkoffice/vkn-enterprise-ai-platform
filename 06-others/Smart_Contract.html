Intelligent Enterprise and Smart customer

Created on 2019-10-13 04:55

Published on 2019-10-13 05:01

Success in business comes from efficient enterprise and a customer who enjoys the value of the enterprise’s outcome. Here I want to detail my experience about making of an intelligent enterprise and transforming a regular customer to smart one!!!

Value based organization

Value creation depends on vision and variation in degree of the value being created. To improve our value creation, it is important to be agile in vision setting. Each vision changes calls for an organization change. Do we have an agile organization which renders the expected value? Can a leader create such an organization by breaking all the cultural and personal mindsets? This is a deal breaker!!!

Intelligence from Information

Continues improvement in value streams is enabled by continues feedback loop. Technology enabled business processes, simplified and mobile enabled check points make process more stable and easier. However, do we get enough feedback in terms of right data? Are we ready to use data as insight?

 Sensors enable periodic and accurate data. Ingesting such data and creating intelligence from it and to use the same for decisions make Information Officer’s life and efficient enterprise.

However, it is always to remember that the data to organization doesn’t only from automated processes. We also should look for parallel simple operation to ensure business continuity.

Optimum security

Security is must. But not at the cost of enterprise efficiency. Imposing constraints to process itself rather than on IT works much better in practice.

Smart customer

Expected ROI and easy operation is a result of being ahead of hurdles in business. It can never happen to business who follow standards and try to be in par with Industry. It takes innovation, being trendy and fashionable and being a champion. It is important to get intelligent vendors to be a smart customer.

 More value from existing investments, Empowered and skilled team and many more to continue….
