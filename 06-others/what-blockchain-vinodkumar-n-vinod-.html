What is Blockchain?

Created on 2018-01-15 05:43

Published on 2018-01-15 06:01

The Blockchain is an incorruptible digital ledger of transactions that can be programmed to record everything of value, especially transactions. It has distributed single source of truth, can arbitrate and execute smart contracts.

Principles of Blockchain are

  • Transactions, but trusted
  • Transactions, but transparent
  • Transactions, but arbitrated
  • Transactions, but Cheap
  • Transactions, but Faster
  • Single source of truth, but distributed
  • Contracts, but Smart
  • Audit trial but tokenized
  • Negotiations, but once only

How do we see Blockchain implementation?

Enterprises are going to continue all the master data, business logic, Analytics and AI as it is now. How ever only transnational data is captured in Blockchain. This data is distributed truth which will be available for all the stake holders.
